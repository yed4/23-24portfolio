Design Beginnings


Design Fuego

This was our very first project in this course. Using source code given by the professor we had to edit html files to make a website that served as an introduction of ourselves. We covered our majors, interest, and what we hoped to get out of this class.

Logo A/B/C

In this project we were asked to design three logos that represented a personal brand or image. We used various different programs like Canvas Logo Maker and Adobe Express. There was a multitude of fonts, layouts colors, and shapes available to create the most representing logos of ourselves.

UX Testing Logo Project

With this project we explored the of inner workings UX Testing and how we can use it to better our logo designs. Using various tools to interview, transcribe and get feedback from different peers, we were able to make modifications to our logos and come up with a final logo that is best representing of oneself.

Cartoon Portrait

For this unit we covered how to turn a picture into a cartoon image. Using adobe illustrator as our main software we navigated the different controls to cartonify the image of ourself we chose.

Good Design

For this project we had to find an image, poster or magazine that we believed represented good design. Once we selected an image of our liking we used Adobe InDesign to enhance the design and magnify the the components of the image that represented proper design principles.

Course Goals


1. Consider the role of rhetoric, technology, and ethics in acts of making.

Rhetoric, technology, and ethics are integral to acts of making. Rhetoric shapes how people convey their intentions and messages, enhancing a creation's impact. Technology provides the tools and methods to bring ideas to life, expanding creative possibilities. It's extremely fascinating to see how far we've come with innovations, like 3D printing,  which can often blur the lines between physical and digital creation. When thinking about ethics it requires creators to assess their impact on society, the environment, and individuals. Learning the skills in a class like this may seem futile to some people but the information provided has the ability to change people’s lives especially in this digital age we live in today.

2. Leverage Design Principles.

Learning about the various principles that make good design, such as contrast, color, typography, has been essential to my journey and work output in this class. Being able to fully understand these concepts and utilize them has been a fun process of trial and error. I am excited to learn about more concepts and see how they shape my future projects in this class.

3. Engage with others in a workshop environment.

Being able to have the time in class to cooperate, help and learn from one another is extremely beneficial and necessary I would say for this course. Looking at other people’s work and having them give feedback on my own work allows me to get tan idea of how other people think and what influences their creative output. This helps me look inward and discern what affects my creative mind and how I can improve on different aspects.



4. Practice revision within and across media forms.

Having had the opportunity to utilize various different design tools and platforms allowed e to learn an array of skills. The most important is utilizing the right softwares to maximize the outcome of the design or object one is trying to make. Making sure that files and documents are in the appropriate format and compatible with different tools is also something I had to learn the hard way. However once mastered and in unison, these platforms can help one create whatever their heart and minds desire.

5. Learn to present your work.

So far the various projects we have been faced with have most definitely helped be able to present my work to an audience better, using different means of communication. The best example would be during our UX Testing phase when we had to find a way to showcase the logo designs to the interviewees and ask them very specific questions to be able to improve on our logos with the given feedback.

Short Form


Two-page Spread

For this project we first started off by exploring two page spreads we belivied represented good design. After that we made our own two page spreads with the use of InDesign software.

Graphic Design Resume

With the help of InDesign we created our own resumes. However instead of going for the professional approach, these reseumes were meant to be a representation of ourselves and how we present ourselves through the lens of design.

Advertisement

Using Adobe Photoshop we were tasked to crete an advertisment for something of our choice. Making sure that all the text, images and components are all cohesive and consitent was quite the learning experience.

Client Project

For this project we had the opportunity to create and design various media for a specific client we were paired. It truly gave me the feeling of being in a professional setting and designing for a client in need of a firms expertise. It gave me alot of insight on how to go about designing things for people other than myself.
